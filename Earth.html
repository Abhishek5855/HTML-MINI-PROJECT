About Earth

Only planet in the solar system to be known to support life.
Has water in all three states – liquid, solid, and gas. It takes 365.26 days to revolve around the Sun.
Gases present in the Earth’s atmosphere are Nitrogen, Hydrogen, Oxygen, Carbon Dioxide, etc.
It takes 23 hours 56 minutes 46 seconds to rotate around its axis.
The Ozone layer present in the Earth’s atmosphere protects it from ultraviolet rays.
The Moon is the only natural satellite of the Earth.
