Mars

About Mars

Known as the ‘Red Planet’ due to the presence of iron-rich red soil.
Despite its red color, it is cold because it has lost most of its atmosphere.
Phobos and Deimos are the two moons of Mars.
Has polar ice caps and traces of sub-terrestrial liquid water have been found.
Has the largest known volcano (and second tallest mountain) in the solar system – Mons Olympus.
Spacecrafts to Mars: Mariner 4, 2001 Mars Odyssey, Mars Orbiter Mission (India), MAVEN, etc.
Rovers on Mars: Opportunity, Curiosity, Spirit (decommissioned).
