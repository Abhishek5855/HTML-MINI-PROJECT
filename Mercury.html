Mercury

About Mercury

Closest planet to the Sun. Orbits the Sun the quickest.
No significant atmosphere. No natural satellites (moons).
Rotation period is longer than its orbital (revolution) period.
Spacecrafts to Mercury: Mariner 10, MESSENGER, BepiColombo (upcoming).
Can be seen from Earth only before sunrise and after sunset.
